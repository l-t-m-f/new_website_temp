My name is Émile Fréchette, I am the creator of the upcoming 2D adventure game Exterminables and the founder of Let the Mice Free, an independant game production house on Montréal island. I am a teacher in video games at Lasalle College.


Before founding my studio, I worked for multiple years in TV and movies, and I was extensively involved in building the video game design educational program for the award-winning charity Youth Fusion. I have more than 10 years of experience making and teaching how to make games.


On my personal website, you can discover more about my multi-disciplinary art background and my projects, including various web games, jams, and prototypes.
